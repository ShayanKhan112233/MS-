The Greedy Dog

One day, a dog stole a piece of meat from a butcher
shop and ran off with it. On his way home, he had
to cross a river, and when he looked down at the
water,The dog was so pleased with his find that
he ran to the river to admire his reflection in the
water. As he gazed into the water, he saw another
dog with a larger bone in his mouth. The greedy
dog wanted that bone too and foolishly snapped
at the image in the water, he saw his own reflection
with the meat in its mouth. Thinking that another
dog had a bigger piece of meat, he foolishly jumped
into the river to take it for himself. As he opened his
mouth to grab the meat, he lost his own piece to the
river's current. The dog went home hungry,
losing his stolen meat due to greed.

Moral:

Don't be greedy, or you may lose everything.
