The Thirsty Crow

In a calm village, there was a smart crow named Cleo.
One super hot day, Cleo got thirsty, just like everyone
else. But the village well had a small opening, making
it hard for Cleo to drink water. Cleo saw a pitcher near

Cleo had an idea. She picked up the stones and

this until the water was high enough for her to take
a nice drink. The villagers saw how clever Cleo was
and gathered around, impressed by her smart move.
The story of the smart crow spread throughout the
village, and everyone felt inspired. After drinking
and impressing the villagers, Cleo flew away.
Her story taught everyone, especially the
students, that even tricky problems can
be solved with a bit of creative thinking.
